1. I'm Thinking Of Ending Things

Movie of 2020. Might possibly the "scariest" movie of the year. Both Jessie Buckley and Jesse Plemons give what I think is the best performances of their careers. Charlie Kaufman has such a weird and deep understanding of humanity and their personal anxities. I love how the subtly details are changed, give it such an errie feel sometimes.

2. Tenet

Really strong lead debut for John David Washington. Man is like a cool black James Bond. Robert Pattison continues to deliver great performances. The chemistry between these two guys are great. I can understand the plot being a little confusing but I think this one of those movies that will age well and gain appreciating as time goes on. The subtle things that Nolan does and is able to explore with the yo-yo like plot progression.
